0 · Four reasons — plain language

Each reason is something a typical IndiaMART listing or Maximiser plan does not fully solve — even at ~₹72,000 including tax per year.

Reason 1
Enquiries come to your email & WhatsApp

Form or WhatsApp on your site → business email (e.g. orders@) and customer tracking (HubSpot). IndiaMART keeps enquiries in their app. If subscription stops, that list is much harder to keep.

Reason 2
Forms built for your industry

IndiaMART: one box — “describe your requirement.” We build fields for your trade — e.g. event type & quantity (awards), or square feet & timeline (modular interiors).

Reason 3
Your brand — no competitor beside you

On IndiaMART, buyers see alternatives on the same screen. On your website, they see only your work, trust badges, and testimonials.

Reason 4
Pay once · own forever · see what works

One-time build (from ₹43,000–48,000) vs yearly marketplace fees. Google Analytics shows which products and buttons bring enquiries.

8 · Words we use (glossary)

IndiaMART listing
Your paid profile on indiamart.com — buyers find you in the marketplace (often ~₹28,000–41,000 per year).
Maximiser plan
IndiaMART’s higher tier — hosted storefront on their system, ~₹72,000 including tax per year.
Enquiry
When a buyer asks for price, quote, or information — not yet a confirmed order.
Business email
Professional address on your domain (e.g. orders@maanvikhandicrafts.com), not only Gmail.
Customer tracking
Recording each enquiry through quote and delivery (we use HubSpot — free tier).
Google Analytics
Free tool showing how many people visit your site and which buttons they use.
Owned website
Site on your domain that you keep after paying — not rented from IndiaMART.
Bulk order
Large institutional order (e.g. college trophies) — often ₹50,000–2,00,000+.
Discovery call
30-minute session to learn your business before we quote modules.
Renewal
When your yearly IndiaMART payment comes due again — a decision point for Path B.
