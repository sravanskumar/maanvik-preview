Positioning: IndiaMART is the discovery standard for Indian B2B. An owned storefront must match its conversion mechanics (trust, per-product quote, browse) while winning on brand, lead ownership, and buyer qualification. Maanvik redesign is the live proof-of-concept.

Long-term vision: emerge as an alternative platform — not a listing board, but owned storefronts with industry-specific enquiry intelligence (events for awards, sq ft/projects for interiors, MOQ for manufacturing).
Why the dual-form pattern matters for your IndiaMART alternative thesis
IndiaMART treats every vertical the same: photo, price, “describe requirement.” Your platform thesis is vertical-aware lead capture — Maanvik (event + quantity), ASR (sq ft + rooms + timeline). That is a defensible product difference, not just prettier HTML.
